I'm Denish Acharya — an international freight and logistics professional currently working at Team Global Logistics Pvt Ltd, Kathmandu. With 1.5+ years of hands-on experience in freight forwarding, I manage ocean and air cargo shipments across Asia's key trade corridors into Nepal.
